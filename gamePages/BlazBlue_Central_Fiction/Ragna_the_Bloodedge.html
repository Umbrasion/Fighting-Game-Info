Ragna the Bloodedge

Overview

PLAYSTYLE: Rushdown

Ragna the Bloodedge is the protagonist, meaning he's the resident "pick me if you want a variety of tools for a variety of options" character. He's fairly simple to grasp, has many good buttons, and is a solid beginner pick for understanding mechanics. He's also edgy looking, if that's your thing. Not that edgy of a guy, though!

Unique Mechanics

Lifesteal
Ragna's D attacks will heal him by a certain amount when they hit or are blocked. This healing gets more potent the more lifesteal moves are used in a combo.

Overdrive (Blood Kain IDEA)
When in Overdrive, Ragna's D attacks become more powerful and are granted increased lifesteal.

PICK IF YOU LIKE:
  • Beginner's Best Friend: Simple, easy character with tools that aren't complex to understand.
  • Came Prepared: Ragna has options that can be used in a variety of situations.
  • Livin' Easy: With lifesteal, Ragna can live longer than some and live something that could have potentially been his end otherwise.
AVOID IF YOU DISLIKE:
  • Gimmickless: Ragna has lifesteal, but that's relatively simple in the realm of fighting game gimmicks.
