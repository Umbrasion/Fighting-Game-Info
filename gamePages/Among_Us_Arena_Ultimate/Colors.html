Red

PLAYSTYLE: Mixup

Unique Moves

  • S
    • Red enters a vent in the ground, then pops out of it to attack. Acts as a low. Good damage and can start combos.
    • Holding down the button will cause Red to move across the stage, only leaving the vent when the button is released or a certain amount of time has passed. Hits from the other side if you hold it for long enough, allowing for left/right mixups.
    • Pressing at any point while you are in the vent will cause Red to drop from the ceiling as an overhead.
    • When used in the air, Red falls quickly down to the ground to enter the vent.
  • EX
    • Costs 2 bars of meter.
    • Invinvible on startup, allowing it to act as a reversal.
    • Does more damage than normal vent and lauches at a slower speed for easier combos.
    • The overhead version, however, has no additional properties, rendering it the same as the normal version.
  • Taunt
    • Red puts their finger to their lips(?) and says "SHHHH!"
    • Has no hitbox or special properties.

Blue

PLAYSTYLE: All-Rounder

Words

Unique Moves

  • S
    • Blue does a double flip forward, swinging their knife around them. Very useful for combos with good damage.
    • When used in the air, the second hit will come out when hitting the ground. Both hits link fairly well.
  • EX
    • Costs 2 bars of meter.
    • Blue spins around for a 4-hit slash attack. Drags opponents to the corner, does a lot of damage, and very useful in combos.
  • Taunt
    • Blue waves their knife, stabbing 3 times. Not especially useful, but funny to catch your opponent with.

Green

PLAYSTYLE: Mixup

Words

Unique Moves

  • S
    • Green swipes their card in an overhead attack. With how fast it comes out, it is very useful for high/low mixups.
  • S (Air)
    • Green spins around and swipes their card all around them. Not as useful as the grounded version, but still somewhat useful in air combos.
  • EX
    • Costs 2 bars of meter.
    • Green leaps into the air and flips around, which hits overhead, positions Green on the opposite side of the opponent, and is useful as a combo tool.
    • Unlike their normal sus, the EX version is the same in the air.
  • Taunt
    • Green sits there like a dope.
    • Has a random chance to play a fart reverb sound effect.

Pink

PLAYSTYLE: Brawler

Words

Unique Moves

  • S
    • Pink rockets straight up with an oxygen tank. Useful as a combo tool for juggling.
    • Links reliably into the air version of itself.
    • Air/ground Sus loops are possible, however they don't last for too many hits, take a bit of timing, and don't do that much damage.
  • S (Air)
    • Pink rockets straight down with an oxygen tank. Useful for juggling combos or quickly getting to the ground when approaching from the air.
    • Links reliably into the grounded version of itself.
    • Air/ground Sus loops are possible, however they don't last for too many hits, take a bit of timing, and don't do that much damage.
  • EX
    • Costs 1 bar of meter.
    • Pink rockets diagonally downward. Much like its normal version, can be used well in combos, with the additional benefit of dragging across the stage.
    • Links reliably into the air version of itself.
    • Has invincibility on startup, making it a useful refersal.
  • EX (Air)
    • Costs 1 bar of meter.
    • Pink rockets diagonally downward. Much like its normal version, can be used well in combos, with the additional benefit of dragging across the stage.
    • Unlike its normal version, however, it does not reliably link into the grounded version of itself.
    • Has invincibility on startup like its grounded version. Matters less in the air, however still has its applications.
  • Taunt
    • Pink sticks out their tongue. Does nothing.
