Reimu Hakurei

Overview

PLAYSTYLE:

Reimu Hakurei is 15.5's simple, well-rounded character with solid abilities across her kit. She lacks any special mechanics or unique attributes of her own, hoever makes up for it with solid tools for offense, setups, and great team synergy with many other characters.

If you're looking for a simple and effective playstyle, Reimu may be for you.

Unique Mechanics



PICK IF YOU LIKE:
  • Fighting Game Protagonist: Being the protagonist, Reimu boasts solid tools for all situations. While she may not be the master of them, she can hold her own.
AVOID IF YOU DISLIKE:
  • No X-Factor: Without any special gimmicks, Reimu plays relatively honest to other characters. She lacks anything that can turn the tides of a game quickly.

Tag Attacks

Melee

Reimu performs her j.6A/66B that additionally launches upwards.

Lane Melee

Reimu performs her j.5A.

Shot

Reimu shoots a short stream of purple talismans at the opponent. Decent for extending bullet combos.

Spellcard

Reimu performs her default spellcard, Spirit Sign "Fantasy Seal". Can get you out of a risky commitment to your master's spellcard, or tack on some additional damage to a spellcard combo.
