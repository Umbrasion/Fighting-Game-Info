Game Mechanics

Meter

Meter refers to the bar at the bottom corner of the screen, which builds up over time as you perform certain actions. Your meter has 4 total bars, and various actions will consume a different amount of bars depending on the move. If playing a match and you move on to the next round, both players' amount of meter will be kept into said round.

The filled meter will normally be a dark green; however, upon having enough meter to perform your EX Move, the filled meter will change to a light green, and a sound effect will play. Your meter will also display "SUPER" when you are able to perform your super, though this does not change the color of the bar nor come with any corresponding sound effect. If you reach the maximum 4 bars of meter, your meter will display "MAX" (which takes precedence over it displaying "SUPER").

Burst

Among Us Arena contains a burst mechanic, which allows you to escape out of any combo at any time. Bursts can be used with 2 bars of meter, however if you have more, it will consume all remaining meter up to a maximum of 3 bars. Bursts can be performed by pressing any attack button + the Sus button.

A small emergency meeting button icon next to your meter lights up to indicate when you are able to burst.

Pushblock

S

Pushblock is an action that can be performed while blocking an attack that pushes your opponent a large distance away from you. This does not cost any resources to use.
