FAQ

What is this site for?
Originally a basic informational place on fighting games made for a tournament series hosted by me and my friend, it's since expanded to something quite large in scope and is now more of a general fighting game resource with the aim to be as welcoming to beginners as possible while also being deep for more experience players.
How long will this take to make?
Dunno! It's mainly based on my own motivation and sway of life. I'm always open to others contributing, though it's certainly more difficult than a public wiki. It's probably something I'll chip away at in my own offtime.
