FAQ

What is this site for?
This site was made as a way to have easily accessible information on the games included in Ready or Not, GAMBLE!
Why not just use Dustloop/Mizuumi/etc.?
I wanted to make something that was a bit more friendly to people who don't know anything about fighting games or are mainly just trying to play to have fun. Also I'm extremely autistic
