Overview

Ready or Not, GAMBLE! is a tournament series where we take craziness to the extreme and let randomness take hold. Every match, the game is different and the way you play is different. The only thing you can prepare for is the unpredicatable!

Rules

The rules for the first RNG are as follows:

  • Every match will have a game that is randomly selected. This cannot be changed.
  • Every game will have a condition randomly selected that will last until the match is over. Both players participating must abide by the condition or they will automatically lose the round.
  • Rulesets are determined on a game-by-game basis. Conditions can change these rules.
  • Games and conditions cannot be rerolled unless either would create issues for any participants.
  • Matches are played exclusively over Parsec. Owning the game is not required. Both players owning the game does not change this rule and will be played on Parsec regardless.
  • Lag tests are not given during the tournament. If a connection check is required, it will be done before tournament time.
