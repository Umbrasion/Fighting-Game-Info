Set Procedure

  • At the start of each set, a tournament organizer will run a random pull to determine the game the players will play. Each game has a set of rules tailored to the game and must be followed by the players (see Game-Specific Rules below for more details).
  • After pulling the game, the tournament organizer will then
  • Both players join the game together and pick their characters. They may choose to divulge
    • Players may switch the character they are playing between rounds, regardless of if the player had lost/won the previous game.
    • If a disallowed character is selected, the game will be paused and the offending player must re-select their character.
  • Stages are either selected from the legal stage list, picked at random, or are mutually decided by both players to play on, depending on the individual game's ruleset.
    • If a disallowed stage is selected, the game will be paused and the stage will be re-selected.
